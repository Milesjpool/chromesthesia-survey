CHROMESTHESIA

About the Chromesthesia Project

As part of the 7digital Tech Academy I am working on an application to categorise music by the colours which they evoke in the mind's eye. The aim being to eventually visualise any customer's locker as a kind of 'mood spectrum'.

To complete my project I am carrying out this survey to determine which colours may be stimulated when listening to a particular piece of music.

• When you press START a short piece of music will start playing (you'll want to grab some headphones first).
• Simply select the colour you feel relates to the current song, and the next piece will start playing.
• Feel free to continue for as long as you like.

Thank you for your time, and any feedback is much appreciated,
